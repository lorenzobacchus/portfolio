Hey! My name is Lorenzo Bacchus, I’m from a small Caribbean island called St. Vincent and the Grenadines and I’m 29 years old.

Since I was a child I have worked alongside my father on different construction sites. Seeing how hard he worked made me develop a passion for Architecture. In my mind, if I were to become an Architect, and start my own firm, I would be able to allow my father to rest.

My architectural beliefs are simple. I believe that buildings should be designed not just for function, but also to inspire people. Additionally, I believe that as technology progresses, so should Architecture. Buildings should be designed with the future in mind, just as technology is.

But, Architecture is not my only interest. I also have a great interest in team sports because they teach you how to be more disciplined, patient, respectful, honest, loving; and they help bridge the communication gap between individuals. When you have fun together doing something you love, all tension and reservations disappear.

In Architecture, these skills become especially important in teaching us how we should respond to a site, its surroundings, and its users.
