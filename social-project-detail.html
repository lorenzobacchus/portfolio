Co-operative Social Housing

The Task: Create a Social Housing design for a historic site. This site holds hundreds of years of memories; from Japan to Germany to Taiwan. The site features a public bath that was considered the Heart of the Village and all areas were connected like a giant town square.

The Response: Create a design that incorporates some of the site's historic features and connect the village to the surrounding community. Create a space where people from all around the village can meet and interact while doing simple crop farming.
