Urban Landscape: Child and Youth Environmental Education and Playable Place

Aurora Skatepark

This design exercise uses the urban landscape as an education and play field for children and young people and provides public activities spaces for Taipei citizens to explore the boundary of urban space activities and redefine the urban architectural space. The theme I chose is the skatepark. It can be seen from the design instructions that if the correct method is used, skateboarding will be a very interesting and educational activity. Therefore, for this project, my focus is to create a space where children and adults can play together, gain life experience and learn new skills. Education should be fun and should always attract the interest of learners!
